Our Story

It Started With a Problem.

Buying a car in Zimbabwe used to be a gamble. Fake mileage, hidden mechanical faults, and shady paperwork were the norm. We wanted to change that.

Zyamutala was born to provide a safe haven for car deals. We believe that every transaction should be transparent, every car should be what it says it is, and every client should walk away satisfied.

"We don't just sell cars. We sell peace of mind in an unpredictable market."
