Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and selling with us.

How does Zyamutala ensure the cars are safe?
We physically inspect every vehicle listed on our platform. We check the mechanical condition, verify ownership documents with Zinara and CVR, and ensure there are no outstanding police clearance issues.
What are your fees?
We charge a flat 5% commission fee on the final sale price. This fee covers our verification services, marketing, and negotiation support. There are no upfront fees to list your car.
Can I test drive a car before buying?
Absolutely. Once you're interested in a vehicle, we arrange a safe and convenient meeting place for you to inspect and test drive the car under our supervision.
Do you offer payment plans?
Currently, we facilitate cash transactions and bank transfers. We are working on partnerships with financial institutions to offer financing options in the future.
